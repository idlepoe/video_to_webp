Privacy Policy

The VideoToWebp app (hereinafter referred to as "the app") values the protection of users' personal information and complies with relevant laws and regulations such as the Personal Information Protection Act.

1. Personal Information Collected

This app is available to everyone without registration and does not collect any personal information directly.

However, this app uses Google AdMob for advertising purposes, and AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version, etc.)
  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads)
  • App usage information
  • Location information (if location permissions are granted)

2. Video File Processing

• Video files are temporarily uploaded to our servers for conversion purposes only
• Files are processed without encryption (no end-to-end encryption)
• Original video files are automatically deleted after conversion (within 24 hours)
• Converted WebP files are deleted from servers after download
• Maximum file size limit is 20MB due to processing costs

3. Purpose of Using Personal Information

This app does not directly collect or use personal information. However, AdMob collects information for the following purposes:

  • Providing personalized advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance and effectiveness
  • Improving ad targeting and user experience
  • Complying with advertising regulations

4. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

This app does not directly retain personal information. AdMob data retention follows Google's privacy policy, typically retaining data for up to 24 months for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

This app does not directly provide personal information to third parties. However, AdMob may share collected information with:

  • Google and its advertising partners
  • Advertisers and ad networks
  • Analytics providers

For more information about AdMob's data practices, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Measures for Personal Information Protection

This app does not directly collect personal information. AdMob data protection is handled by Google according to their security standards and privacy policies.

7. User Rights

This app does not directly collect personal information. For AdMob data, users can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads through device settings
  • Reset their advertising ID
  • Contact Google regarding their data rights
